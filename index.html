What you need to know about me:


I have not lost my curioisty. Which is why most of the things I enjoy (reading, puzzles, hikes, baking), all require some form of thinking.

What is the plot? What did he mean by that? Was the author foreshadowing something (8 times of 10, it's yes, haha)? Do any of these pieces share a sliver of the same color? What's over there? Is the ground more worn in here or there? I wonder what would happen if I substituted melted butter for softened butter? What if I added only white sugar this time? Does the order matter? Can I throw everything in the bowl and have the same result?

The point? Well that's really up to you to find out. Is there a correlation between any of this and a good worker? Try to collect some data. I am more than happy to be part of the study ;)

Jokes aside, I am always eager to keep learning and growing as a professional and individual.
